The Story Behind Conan Edogawa

From Shinichi Kudo to Conan Edogawa

Conan Edogawa, originally known as Shinichi Kudo, was a brilliant high school detective who was transformed into a child after being poisoned by members of the Black Organization. To protect his identity and continue solving cases, he adopted the alias Conan Edogawa, combining the names of two famous mystery writers: Arthur Conan Doyle and Edogawa Rampo.

The Black Organization

The Black Organization is a mysterious criminal syndicate involved in various illegal activities. Their main objective is developing a powerful drug called APTX 4869, which unexpectedly caused Shinichi's transformation.

Main Characters

Ai Haibara

Ai Haibara

A former member of the Black Organization who was also transformed into a child. She's a brilliant scientist and Conan's ally, providing crucial support in investigations.

Conan Edogawa

Conan Edogawa

The main protagonist, a brilliant high school detective who was transformed into a child. Despite his small size, he solves complex cases with his sharp mind and deductive skills.

Ran Mouri

Ran Mouri

Conan's childhood friend and love interest. A skilled martial artist with a kind heart, she often helps Conan in his investigations while unaware of his true identity.

Kogoro Mouri

Kogoro Mouri

A private detective and Ran's father. Known as the "Sleeping Detective," he often solves cases with Conan's help, though he's unaware of Conan's true identity.

Heiji Hattori

Heiji Hattori

A high school detective from Osaka and Conan's rival-turned-friend. He's one of the few people who knows Conan's true identity and often helps in solving cases.

Kaito Kid

Kaito Kid

A mysterious phantom thief known for his spectacular heists and magic tricks. Despite being a thief, he often helps Conan in dangerous situations.

Sonoko Suzuki

Sonoko Suzuki

Ran's best friend and a wealthy heiress. She is known for her bold personality and often gets involved in Conan's adventures.

Dr. Hari

Dr. Hari

A brilliant scientist and a friend of the Mouri family. He often provides technical support and gadgets to Conan for his investigations.

Directors

Kenji Kodama

Known for directing several Detective Conan movies including "The Time Bombed Skyscraper" and "The Fourteenth Target". His work has been praised for its dynamic action sequences and emotional storytelling.

Yasuichiro Yamamoto

Directed "The Last Wizard of the Century" and "Captured in Her Eyes". His films are known for their intricate mysteries and character development.

Masato Sato

Directed "Countdown to Heaven" and "The Phantom of Baker Street". His work is characterized by its suspenseful atmosphere and innovative storytelling.

Nana Harada

Directed "Crossroad in the Ancient Capital" and "Strategy Above the Depths". Her films are known for their beautiful visuals and emotional depth.

Susumu Mitsunaka

Directed "The Private Eyes' Requiem" and "Jolly Roger in the Deep Azure". His work is praised for its action-packed sequences and engaging plotlines.

Kobun Shizuno

Directed "The Raven Chaser" and "The Lost Ship in the Sky". His films are known for their thrilling action scenes and complex mysteries.
